1. .gitattributes

   Example: *.ts text eol=lf

   Kya hota hai: node_modules, .env, build files track nahi hote
   Requirement: ✅ MUST HAVE
   Example: node_modules/, .env, dist/

   Location: Root

   Requirement: ✅ MUST HAVE
   Example: API_BASE_URL=http://localhost:3552/v1/api/
