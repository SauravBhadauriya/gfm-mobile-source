1. OVERVIEW
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
Build a professional networking platform for creators within Gully Fame,
similar to LinkedIn but focused on talent, credibility, and work history.

KEY PRINCIPLES:
- Professional, calm, and credible (NOT social media-like)
- Focus on talent and work history
- No public comments feed
- No follower-count obsession
- No entertainment algorithm

TARGET USERS:
- Creators (musicians, dancers, comedians, etc.)
- Brands/Recruiters (verified accounts)
- Event organizers
